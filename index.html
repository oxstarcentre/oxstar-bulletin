Teaching highlights
 
  • Medical students: We're delighted to welcome Dr Ashley Tomlinson officially to the role of Clinical Lecturer; a huge thanks to his predecessorDr Nathan Gauge for his work and leadership.
  • FY & OCC: guideline‑current, MDT scenarios score top marks—“Best sim session I have done.” Great entry point if you’d like to teach.
  • VR in focus: award‑winning paediatric VR continues to grow; VR research is now a core strand.
Research snapshot
 
We’re scaling fast: 5 DPhil, 2 DM, 1 ACF and 2 Master’s students across clinical education and human factors/patient safety. Active projects span VR, reflective practice, decision‑making, digital consent, social networks, AI‑assisted decision‑making, GP emergency cognitive aids, and incident‑investigation quality tools.
